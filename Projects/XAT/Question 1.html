HOW TO USE XAT

How to create a table

In the "Input number of rows" section at the top left of your screen, you put the number of row you want your table to have and click on the Create table row(s) button

How to Import a table

To import a table from Microsoft Word or Excel, etc, simply copy the table you wish to import and paste it in the black space above the "Run in XAT button" Note: the table must contain numbers only, (no special characters) and it must have a minimum of 4 columns and a maximum of 5. After pasting the table, click the "Run in XAT button" Also: if you want to import multiple tables, you must copy then paste then click the "Run in XAT button" before you copy the next table

How to Export a table

If you are done with your calculations and you wish to export your work to an external source like Microsoft Word or Excel, simply click on the "Convert to Table button." It will create a table containing all the necessary data which you can then copy and paste where ever you wish. To copy the table, simply left click and hold then drag your cursor along until you have highlighted all you wish to copy then press Ctrl+C to copy or right click and chose the copy option.
